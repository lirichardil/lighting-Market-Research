### DESIGN SERVICES — OUR PROCESS

**Headline:** `How We Work`
**Intro:** `Every project follows the same four-phase structure. No handoffs to other firms. No surprises after installation.`

---

**Phase 01 — Consultation**
> We start by listening. Tell us about your space, your project timeline, your aesthetic direction, and your budget range. We'll come back with an honest scope assessment and a fixed-fee proposal.
> *Duration: 1 meeting + 48-hour turnaround on proposal*

**Phase 02 — Design & Specification**
> We design the lighting environment layer by layer — ambient, task, accent — and produce a full specification: fixture schedule, CCT (color temperature) selections, dimming zones, and AV/controls integration where applicable. You review and approve before anything is ordered.
> *Deliverables: lighting layout drawings, fixture schedule, controls schematic, CCT sample review*

**Phase 03 — Procurement & Coordination**
> We procure all specified fixtures directly — no substitutions without your written approval. We coordinate with your electrician, contractor, and AV integrator. If there's a lead time issue, you hear about it from us before it becomes a problem.
> *Deliverables: purchase orders, delivery tracking, trade coordination schedule*

**Phase 04 — Installation & Sign-Off**
> We're on-site for installation to ensure fixture placement matches the design drawings. We conduct a final walkthrough with you before closing the project. If something isn't right, we fix it — not bill for it.
> *Deliverables: installation sign-off sheet, as-built fixture schedule, warranty documentation*

### CUSTOM MANUFACTURING

**Headline:** `Your Design. Our Manufacturing.`
**Subheadline:** `Have a fixture concept that doesn't exist? We'll build it — from prototype to production run, with full quality control and direct shipping to your project site.`

---

**Who this is for**
> **For designers:** You've specced a custom fixture that a manufacturer won't take on at your project scale. We handle minimum-order realities.
>
> **For developers and hospitality groups:** You need a signature fixture across 200 hotel rooms — consistent CCT, consistent finish, consistent quality. We've seen what inconsistency across a production run costs. We prevent it.
>
> **For homeowners with a vision:** You saw something — a piece of art, an antique, a reference image — and want it made into a light. We can work from a sketch, a photo, or a napkin drawing.

---

**How custom manufacturing works (5 steps)**

**Step 01 — Design Submission**
> Share your concept with us. A sketch, a reference image, a CAD file, a mood board — whatever level of development you're at. We review and respond within 3 business days with questions, clarifications, and a rough feasibility assessment.

**Step 02 — Technical Development**
> Our team works with your design to develop production-ready documentation: materials, finishes, light source specifications, electrical standards (UL/ETL compliance for US installation), and packaging requirements.

**Step 03 — Prototype**
> We produce a physical prototype before committing to production. You review the prototype — finish, light quality, weight, dimensions — and approve or request adjustments.

**Step 04 — Production**
> Once approved, we move to full production run. Minimum quantities depend on the complexity of the design. We conduct QC at the factory and provide shipment tracking from China to your site.

**Step 05 — Delivery & Documentation**
> Every unit ships with full UL/ETL compliance documentation, spec sheets, and installation instructions in English.

---

**What we can manufacture**
- Pendant and suspension fixtures
- Wall sconces and bath fixtures
- Architectural ceiling fixtures
- Custom decorative fixtures in metal, glass, stone, wood, or mixed materials
- Outdoor fixtures with appropriate IP ratings

---

**FAQ**

*What is the minimum order quantity?*
> It depends on the design. Simple designs may start at 10–20 units. Complex custom work typically requires 50+ units to be cost-effective. We'll give you an honest assessment upfront.

*How long does it take?*
> Prototype: 4–6 weeks from design approval. Production: 8–14 weeks from prototype approval, depending on volume and complexity.

*Can you match a specific color temperature or finish?*
> Yes. We verify CCT at source and can match finishes to a physical sample you provide.

*Do the fixtures comply with US electrical standards?*
> Yes. All production fixtures are manufactured to UL/ETL standards required for licensed US electrician installation.

*Can I get samples before ordering?*
> Yes. Sample cost and timeline vary by design. Ask during your consultation.
